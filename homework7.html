Homework 7: Illustrated Environment

The photo I used for this project was taken about two years ago when I went hiking. I chose this image because I remember the scene clearly and I wanted to try turning a real memory into a simple illustration. I imported the photo into Adobe Illustrator on my iPad and I tried to simplify the shapes and colors while still keeping the feeling of the original place. I know my final illustration is not perfect. Controlling the tools on the iPad was a bit difficult, especially with the lines and color blending, but I did my best to adjust and fix the colors.
