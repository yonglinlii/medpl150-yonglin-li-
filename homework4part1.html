Why this picture??

This photomontage combines three different moments and places. The background photo of me in the blue dress was taken in Cape May, New Jersey, on a very sunny and hot day. The photo of my hand holding the camera was shot in my classroom. The image displayed inside the camera was taken a few days earlier by Angelene in the school auditorium. By merging these three photos, I created a single image that brings together very different settings. My goal with this photomontage is to show how the images we see through a camera are not always real. In todayâ€™s digital world, many things we see in photos can be edited or staged. By combining and manipulating these pictures, I wanted to remind viewers not to fully believe everything in a photograph, what appears on the screen may not be the full truth!!!
